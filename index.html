Interests

Computational Social Science: I am particularly interested in social media analysis and human behavior understanding. Using and analyzing data types found in social media such as text, photos, and video and the emotions and sentiments associated with these data can improve social media monitoring and exploration. Getting to know different users and communities on social media is very intriguing, and machine learning can be used to examine people’s psychological characteristics and interests.

Explainable AI: Among the research topics I am eager to pursue and learn more about are understandable and explainable machine learning and deep learning. Since I have worked in many interdisciplinary projects, explaining the model and results has always been challenging for me, and I have tried explaining the output to others by visualizing and analyzing the data and results, which is why I am interested in this topic.

Fairness in AI: Fairness in AI and eliminating bias, particularly social bias, is one of my favorite research topics. It has occurred several times in my projects, and I have attempted to reduce it. An example is improving the algorithm's interpretability or refining the primary data. As artificial intelligence is being used more widely in all industries, it is increasingly critical to consider fairness and eliminate bias, and I am eager to be a part of this field.
