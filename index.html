Selected Projects

Multi-modal Fake News Detection

We conducted research by scrutinizing news articles shared on social media and news outlets. Our aim was to detect fake news by analyzing both the written content and accompanying images. We used advanced machine learning techniques such as embedding images and text, concatenation vectors, and learning specific features of text and images to achieve this.
Code More

Transfer Learning: Through my exploration, the conceptual and semantic embedding techniques were the most effective for combining text and image. Huggingface's BERT model for text and ViT model for image achieved the best results.
Multi-modal Learning: After thorough evaluation of various algorithms, we discovered that for short texts, the Contrastive Loss function combined with concatenation proved to be more effective in fusing text and picture vectors. On the other hand, attention-based fusion worked best for lengthy texts.

Automatic text classification

This project has implemented techniques and models to enhance text classification. It offers an embedding and classification model which can be applied to text through a command. The preliminary analysis of the data is presented before the training results are analyzed. Optuna is used to adjust various parameters and obtain the best possible outcome after selecting the model.

Code More

EDA: During this phase, we carefully analyze the experimental operations, taking into account factors such as the length of the text and the distribution of labels. This helps us ensure that our approach is thorough and accurate, providing the best possible results for our users.
ugmentation: During this phase, there are a variety of techniques available to augment words, documents, and features. These techniques can be utilized to enhance the overall effectiveness of the process without altering the meaning of the original text. It is important to consider these options to ensure the best possible outcome.
Embedding: During this stage, We utilize text embedding algorithms such as Fasttext, Word2vec, and contextual embedding in order to extract essential features.
Algorithms: In order to optimize outcomes, we utilize cutting-edge machine learning, deep learning, and transformer learning models for selection and implementation in our training. Furthermore, we meticulously fine-tune the algorithm parameters to ensure optimal performance.

Bank System through Blockchain

The purpose of this project is to leverage blockchain structure in bank transactions. Each customer's bank account and cash are represented through wallets. Users can create an account and request a loan, which the bank will approve and transfer. The bank head is responsible for regulating and monitoring credit transactions, all of which are executed through blockchain blocks. Security measures have been put in place to ensure the safety of these transactions..

Code More

Security measures: This project has implemented multiple layers of security to guarantee the safety of the blockchain network. These include authentication through the use of the SHA512 hash function, transaction RSA encryption, and a user authentication method.
Network: The functioning of the system is similar to that of a network, with banks, consumers, and servers all serving as nodes. Transactions are then exchanged among these nodes.

As part of a joint effort between the Schools of Computer Science and Civil Engineering, we conducted a study to observe and document the actions of a kindergarten driver. I was a member of technical team, tasked with mapping and analyzing the GPS data obtained from the driver's phone..
